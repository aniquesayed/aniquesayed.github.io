I am a solutions architect working closely with data management platforms (DMPs) in the cyber security field. I work to make the world a better place through the use and ability of technology.

From the first day, I instantly discovered my love for software development and became exposed to many different languages and design and developement paradigms including object-orientated programming (OOP) , modular programming and unified modelling language (UML) design .

More recently, I have positioned myself in the cyber security/ big data world as an architect. I learnt very quickly I like being involved with the vision and strategy and bringing that strategy to life.

This website is here to show my love for the digital world and what I get up to in my spare time - which is very hard to come by these days!
